Reducing Fake Accounts on [Platform Name]

A case study on improving platform integrity and user experience by tackling the challenge of fake accounts.

1. Introduction

This project addresses the pervasive issue of fake accounts on [Platform Name] in [Year], focusing on the strategic efforts of a dedicated "tiger team." As the lead designer, my role involved spearheading design solutions that significantly mitigated this problem.

The initiatives resulted in a remarkable 12% reduction in accepted fake account invites and played a pivotal role in shaping [Platform Name]'s long-term product strategy around user verification.

2. The What and Why

Fake accounts continue to be a significant challenge across all social platforms, including [Platform Name]. While AI offers initial detection, it often falls short in identifying sophisticated fraudulent profiles, necessitating human-centered design interventions.

This section explores the various manifestations of fake accounts and the critical need for thoughtful design to empower users to make informed decisions about their connections.

3. Effective Decision Making & Approach

Our approach involved breaking down silos between product, engineering, and design teams. We adopted the RAPID framework to streamline decision-making and ensure efficient progress.

Tiger Team Roles:

  • Responsible (R): Product Manager
  • Approve (A): Lead Designer (me)
  • Perform (P): Engineering Team
  • Input (I): Data Science, Trust & Safety
  • Decide (D): Senior Leadership

Initial Hypotheses:

  • Adding friction to suspicious connection requests will reduce acceptance rates.
  • Providing more objective information about profiles will help users identify fakes.

4. What Got Shipped

We implemented several key features to combat fake accounts:

Contextual safety interventions for suspicious accounts

We designed interventions to appear when users interact with invitations from suspicious accounts, particularly those lacking mutual connections. This aimed to introduce a necessary "friction" point, prompting users to reconsider before accepting.

Users would see a clear alert when attempting to connect or message such profiles, guiding them towards a safer interaction or decision.

Additional signals with "About this profile"

To empower users with more data, we introduced a feature that provides objective facts about a profile. This "About this profile" section, accessible via the hamburger menu, displays crucial information like profile creation date and last update.

This transparency helps users quickly assess the legitimacy of a profile at a glance.

5. Impact and Learning

The project successfully achieved a 12% reduction in accepted fake account invites, demonstrating the effectiveness of our design interventions.

Key Learnings:

  • Project & Stakeholder Management: The importance of clear communication and alignment across diverse teams.
  • Leadership & Communication: How consistent, transparent updates can drive project momentum.
  • Opportunity for Impact: Even small design changes can lead to significant positive outcomes for platform integrity and user trust.
