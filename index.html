I've pursued unconventional paths to unlock my potential, delivering impactful outcomes for businesses along the way. My passion for engineering lies in its ability to create solutions that amplify human potential in a way that harmonizes with nature - adaptive, sustainable, and non-discriminatory. I believe in designing systems and products that enhance capabilities and align with the inherent fairness and balance of the natural world.
What is your problem-solving mantra?
Clarity builds confidence. I dive into the "unknown unknowns," question the "known unknowns," and focus on "why" to reach "what" and "how." Documenting my thoughts helps solidify my approach.
What's one question you'd ask yourself?
What drives you professionally? I'm driven by curiosity, innovation, and a desire to leave a meaningful impact through my work while being human in every aspect.
